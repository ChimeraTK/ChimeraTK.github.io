struct ChimeraTK::TypeChangingRangeCheckingDecorator< T, IMPL_T >::BadNumericCastException< BOOST_EXCEPTION_T >

Internal exceptions to overload the what() function of the boost exceptions in order to fill in the variable name.

These exceptions are not part of the external interface and cannot be caught explicitly because they are protected. Catch boost::numeric::bad_numeric_cast and derrivatives if you want to do error handling.
